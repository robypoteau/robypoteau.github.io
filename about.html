About Me

How do you write about yourself? What is it’s actual purpose? Is it to give the impression that my qualifications mean something before you meet me? Is it to give a clever and/or geniune idea of who I am? It is a vestige of personalized website? Who knows? Lol

I guess the fact that I started my “About Me” section by asking some questions is already indicative of what I’m like, that is, inquisitive. Additionally, I like to share my thoughts, receive feedback, and synthesize new ideas. This lead me to consider content creation. Though it was a scary proposition, given the virulent nature of online discourse, author Brené Brown helped me find courage.

So, I put together this website and some blogs to record and share my thoughts. I find value in recording myself and I figure there’s a high probability that there are others who also find value in it.

Funny, I guess I went for the clever/genuine “About Me”. Well, at least, I think it’s clever. :)
